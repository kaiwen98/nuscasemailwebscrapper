Braddell Heights Community Club

Fulfill your heart’s desire at Braddell Heights CC. Whether you wish to satisfy your passion for the arts, music and culture or improve your culinary skills, the CC has a wide range of activities and courses for you.

Come sample some of our socio-cultural activities, such as monthly movie screenings exclusively for PAssion Card members, Chinese cultural workshops, Symphony and Chinese Orchestra interest groups and our highly popular men’s cooking interest group.

Call to find out more about the services and facilities available.
